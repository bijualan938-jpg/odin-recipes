Chicken Biriyani

Description

Chicken Biryani is a fragrant rice dish made with marinated chicken, aromatic spices, herbs, and basmati rice. It is one of the most popular dishes in Indian cuisine and is known for its rich flavor and irresistible aroma.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 2 onions (thinly sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies
  • Fresh coriander leaves
  • Fresh mint leaves
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 4 cloves
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • 4 cardamom pods
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 tbsp cooking oil or ghee

Steps

  1. Wash and soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, chili powder, garam masala, and salt for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Heat oil or ghee in a large pot and fry the onions until golden brown.
  4. Add whole spices, tomatoes, and green chilies, then cook until the tomatoes soften.
  5. Add the marinated chicken and cook until it is almost done.
  6. Cook the soaked rice until it is about 70% cooked and drain the water.
  7. Layer the rice over the chicken, then sprinkle mint, coriander, and fried onions on top.
  8. Cover the pot tightly and cook on low heat for 20–25 minutes.
  9. Gently mix before serving and enjoy with raita or salad.
